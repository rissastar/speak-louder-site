Zayah: My Heart in a Dog’s Body

Pitbulls aren’t the problem—ignorance is. Zayah has shown me more love and loyalty than most humans ever have.

My Story with Zayah

When I got Zayah, I was in a rough place. I needed stability, loyalty, and unconditional love—and she gave me all of that without hesitation. She’s not just a dog—she’s my emotional support, my protector, and my family. Despite everything pitbulls are wrongly accused of, Zayah has done nothing but love deeply and fiercely. She’s helped me heal from trauma and taught me what it means to be trusted again.

Myths vs Facts About Pitbulls

  • Myth: Pitbulls are naturally aggressive.
    Fact: Any dog can be aggressive if mistreated. Pitbulls are actually very loyal and people-loving.
  • Myth: Pitbulls have “locking jaws.”
    Fact: That’s completely false. No dog has a mechanism that locks their jaws.
  • Myth: Pitbulls are not safe around children.
    Fact: Pitbulls are known as “nanny dogs” for their gentleness with kids.
