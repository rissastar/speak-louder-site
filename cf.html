What is Cystic Fibrosis?
Cystic Fibrosis (CF) is a genetic disorder that affects the lungs and digestive system by producing thick, sticky mucus that causes blockages and infections.

Is there a cure?
There is currently no cure for CF, but medications like Trikafta have significantly improved quality of life and life expectancy.

What are the daily treatments like?
Daily care includes inhaled medications, airway clearance, enzymes before eating, and frequent clinic visits.
