About Me

I'm Larissa — an advocate, storyteller, and dog mom living with Cystic Fibrosis and cirrhosis. I created this site not just to tell my story, but to give others a voice. Whether you’re here to learn, relate, or speak up, you belong.

This is a space for awareness, healing, and empowerment — a place where we speak louder and love harder, together.
