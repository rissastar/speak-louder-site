About Me

“I’ve faced a lot—but I’ve never stopped fighting to find my voice.”

Hi, I’m Larissa

I’m a Nova Scotia girl with a story full of struggles, survival, and strength. I'm living with Cystic Fibrosis and cirrhosis, and I’m a proud dog mom to a beautiful Pitbull named Zayah.

Through everything—from growing up in foster care to battling addiction and navigating abuse—I’ve learned one thing: there is power in speaking up. That’s what this space is for.

Why I Built This Site

I wanted to create a place where people could learn, connect, and feel seen. Whether you’re here because you’re facing something heavy, or just want to understand more about CF, mental health, or foster care—thank you for being here.

This isn’t just a website. It’s my heart, my story, and a place for others to share theirs too.

Let’s Change the Conversation

I believe in advocacy, awareness, and authenticity. You'll find facts and resources here—but also raw, real-life truths. I want to break the stigma around things people are afraid to talk about.

If my voice shakes something in you, stay. Share. Speak louder and love harder with me.
