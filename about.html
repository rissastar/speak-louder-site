📅 May 28, 2025 – Turning Pain Into Power

Some days, I don’t feel strong. I feel tired, heavy, worn out. But then I remember how far I’ve come—and why I keep going. Every scar, every story, every moment I’ve survived has built the person I am today. I share these pieces of me so someone else out there can feel seen too. Because pain doesn’t define us—but what we do with it does.
