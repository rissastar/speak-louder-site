Facts About Sexual Assault

  • 1 in 3 women and 1 in 6 men experience sexual violence in their lifetime.
  • Most sexual assaults are committed by someone the survivor knows.
  • Only a fraction of sexual assaults are reported to the police.
  • No one ever "asks for it"—clothes, alcohol, or behavior are never consent.
  • Consent must be clear, coherent, willing, and ongoing.

If You’ve Been Assaulted

Please know that what happened to you was not your fault. You deserve to be heard, believed, and supported. Here are some resources where you can get help:

  • Ending Violence Canada
  • RAINN (U.S. National Sexual Assault Hotline)
  • Nova Scotia Sexual Violence Services
  • Talk to someone you trust. Call a local shelter or support center.
