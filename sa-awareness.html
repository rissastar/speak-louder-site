Breaking the Silence

Sexual assault is a traumatic and deeply personal experience that far too many people endure in silence. This page is a space to raise awareness, offer support, and stand with survivors.

What is Sexual Assault?

Sexual assault includes any non-consensual sexual act. It can happen to anyone, regardless of gender, age, or background. It is never the survivor's fault.

What You Can Do:

  • Believe Survivors: Listen without judgment.
  • Speak Out: Challenge rape culture and victim-blaming language.
  • Support Resources: Share crisis lines, therapy services, and local shelters.
